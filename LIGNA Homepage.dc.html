05 Material Story

The wood decides
before we do

Every plank is chosen by hand, dried for months, and read like a text — its grain determines what it will become.

Teak

Grown slowly in Java, dense with natural oils. It darkens gracefully and outlives its makers.

Oak

Pale, calm and even — the quiet foundation of every light-filled room.

Walnut

Deep, figured, unrepeatable. No two boards are ever the same.

06 Craftsmanship

Four generations
of Jepara hands

Jepara has carved wood for five centuries. Our workshop carries that lineage — no shortcuts, no veneers, joinery cut by hand and finished with oil, not lacquer.
